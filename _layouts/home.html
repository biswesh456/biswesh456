Biswesh Mohapatra

I am a Research Engineer at Inria, Paris working with the Articulab group under Prof. Justine Cassell. I am currently working on Socially Aware Embodied Conversational Agents. I have done Integrated Master of Technology (B.Tech + M.Tech) majoring in Computer Science and Engineering at IIIT Bangalore, India.

I have a keen interest on working in the field of Conversational AI and Dialog Systems. I want to incorporate prior knowledge from structural information(like Knowledge Graphs) into our dialog models to make them better at common sense reasoning. I am also interested in looking at the non-verbal behaviours during the conversations and use them to solve problems like grounding, intersubjectivity and progression in dialog systems. Besides this, I have experience working on Machine Translations, Knowledge Graphs and Ontologies.

For my Master's thesis, I worked on a project on incorporating Ontological Information into embeddings for faster reasoning under Prof. G. Srinivasaraghavan in collaboration with IBM Research AI(Sumit Bhatia) and Knowledgable Computing and Reasoning Lab.
